**Role**: product-strategist  
**Type**: Standard (30 min)  
**Priority**: MEDIUM

**Objective**:
Refine pricing strategy with clear discount policies, annual billing incentives, and enterprise custom pricing guidelines to empower sales team.

Unit economics defined base pricing (€199-999), but GTM strategy needs operational pricing rules for sales execution. Sales team needs authority matrices and competitive response guidelines.

**Deliverables**:
1. **Pricing Decision Matrix** (400+ words)
   - By customer segment: Startup, Growth, Enterprise
   - By deal size: Self-serve, Assisted, Sales-led
   - Framework for custom pricing approvals

2. **Discount Authority Guidelines** (300+ words)

3. **Enterprise Pricing Calculator** (200+ words)
   - Base price: €999/month

4. **Competitive Pricing Response Playbook** (200+ words)
   - Vanta comparison: Emphasize EU advantage, not price
   - Drata comparison: Match on features, win on localization
   - Secureframe comparison: Undercut on EU-specific features
   - When to walk away: Price-only buyers
