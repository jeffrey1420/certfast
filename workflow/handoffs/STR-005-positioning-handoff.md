## 🎯 Key Decisions Made

1. **France-First Market Strategy**: Selected French B2B SaaS as P0 segment due to language moat (78% prefer French tools), regulatory sensitivity (GDPR origin), and lack of dominant local player.

2. **Four-Pillar Positioning**: Established AI-Native, European DNA, Founder Economics, and Technical UX as defensible differentiation against Vanta/Drata.

3. **Anti-Positioning Clarification**: Explicitly defined what CertFast doesn't do (checkbox compliance, non-technical companies, white-glove enterprise consulting) to prevent scope creep.

4. **90-Day Core Promise**: Validated speed as #1 decision factor; made "From zero to SOC 2 in 90 days" the primary messaging hook.

5. **Transparent Pricing as Differentiator**: Positioned public pricing and monthly billing as key competitive advantages against opaque, annual-only competitors.
