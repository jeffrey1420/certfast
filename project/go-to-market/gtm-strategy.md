## Executive Summary

This document defines CertFast's comprehensive go-to-market strategy, translating our positioning as "the AI-native compliance platform for European technical founders" into actionable plans for customer acquisition, channel development, and revenue growth. The strategy is designed to achieve €1.2M ARR in Year 1 through a phased launch approach targeting French B2B SaaS companies first.

## 2. Channel Strategy

### 2.1 Channel Mix by Tier

| Channel | Lite | Starter | Pro | Enterprise | Primary Focus |
|---------|------|---------|-----|------------|---------------|
| **Product-Led Growth** | 60% | 10% | 0% | 0% | Self-serve onboarding, viral loops |
| **Partnerships (CPA)** | 20% | 35% | 40% | 40% | Referral network, co-selling |
| **Content/Organic** | 15% | 40% | 25% | 10% | SEO, thought leadership |
| **Outbound Sales** | 0% | 10% | 25% | 40% | ABM, SDR campaigns |
| **Paid Acquisition** | 5% | 5% | 10% | 10% | LinkedIn, retargeting |

### 2.2 Channel Deep Dive

#### Product-Led Growth (PLG)

**Strategy**: Make CertFast discoverable, try-able, and buy-able without human intervention.

**Tactics**:
- Free 14-day trial with full platform access
- Self-serve onboarding with interactive tutorials
- In-app guidance and compliance readiness scoring
- Viral loops: referral credits for successful customer introductions
- Integration marketplace for popular dev tools (GitHub, AWS, GCP)

**Success Metrics**:
- Time to first value: <2 hours
- Trial-to-paid conversion: >20%
- Viral coefficient: >0.3

#### Partnerships (CPA Firms)

**Strategy**: Build a referral network of 10+ European CPA firms who recommend CertFast to their clients.

**Partner Profile**:
- Boutique to mid-size firms (10-50 auditors)
- Focus on SaaS/tech clients
- Strong presence in France, Germany, or UK
- Forward-thinking on technology adoption

**Value Proposition for Partners**:
- 15% revenue share on Year 1 contracts
- Faster audit completion (reduced client friction)
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Early access to new features

**Partner Activation Plan**:
- Month 1-2: Sign 3 anchor partners in France
- Month 3-4: Expand to 6 partners (add Germany)
- Month 6-12: Scale to 10+ partners across EU

#### Direct Sales

**Strategy**: Build consultative sales capability for Pro and Enterprise tiers.

**Sales Motion**:
- Inbound: Demo requests → qualification → proposal → close
- Outbound: Targeted ABM to Series A/B companies
- Sales cycle: 30 days (Pro), 60 days (Enterprise)

**Team Structure (by Month 12)**:
- 1 VP Sales
- 2 Account Executives (FR, DE)
- 2 SDRs
- 1 Sales Engineer

#### Content & Organic

**Strategy**: Own the "European compliance" search category through authoritative content.

**Content Pillars**:
1. Framework guides (SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR)
2. EU regulatory updates (NIS2, DORA)
3. Technical implementation guides
4. Customer success stories
5. Comparison content (vs. Vanta, Drata)

**Distribution**:
- SEO-optimized blog (2 articles/week)
- LinkedIn thought leadership
- GitHub documentation and examples
- Developer community engagement (Hacker News, Reddit)

## 3. Sales Motion

### 3.1 Self-Serve Motion

**Target**: Lite tier customers (<15 employees)

**Journey**:
1. Discover via content, search, or referral
2. Start free trial (no credit card required)
3. Connect infrastructure via guided setup
4. Receive AI-generated policies and evidence collection
5. Upgrade to paid when ready for audit

**Support Model**:
- Chat-based support (Zendesk/Intercom)
- Extensive documentation and video tutorials
- Community forum for peer support
- Email support with 24-hour response SLA

**Pricing**: €199/month or €1,990/year (16.7% discount)

### 3.2 Assisted Motion

**Target**: Starter tier customers (15-50 employees)

**Journey**:
1. Request demo or start trial
2. Discovery call (30 min) to understand needs
3. Custom onboarding plan presented
4. Guided implementation with customer success
5. Quarterly check-ins for expansion

**Support Model**:
- Dedicated onboarding specialist
- 4-hour response SLA
- Monthly office hours
- Priority chat support

**Pricing**: €299/month or €2,990/year

### 3.3 Sales-Led Motion

**Target**: Pro and Enterprise tiers (50+ employees)

**Journey**:
1. Demo request or outbound outreach
2. Discovery call with AE (understanding technical environment, compliance needs)
3. Technical deep-dive with Sales Engineer
4. Custom proposal with implementation timeline
5. Procurement and security review
6. Closed-won with dedicated CSM handoff

**Support Model**:
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager
- 1-hour response SLA
- Quarterly business reviews
- Executive business reviews (Enterprise)

**Pricing**:
- Pro: €499/month or €4,990/year
- Enterprise: €999/month or €9,990/year (custom pricing available)

### 3.4 Motion Transition Points

| Trigger | Self-Serve → Assisted | Assisted → Sales-Led |
|---------|----------------------|----------------------|
| **Company Size** | >15 employees detected | >50 employees detected |
| **Framework Complexity** | Single framework | Multiple frameworks |
| **Integration Count** | <5 integrations | >10 integrations |
| **Support Requests** | >3 tickets in 7 days | Security questionnaire received |
| **Usage Pattern** | Approaching trial limits | Audit timeline <60 days |

## 7. Conclusion

CertFast's go-to-market strategy leverages three core advantages:

1. **Product-Led Growth**: AI-native platform enables rapid self-serve value realization
2. **European Positioning**: First-mover advantage in underserved EU compliance market
3. **Partner Network**: CPA firm relationships provide trusted referral channel

By executing this phased approach—starting with France, validating channels, then expanding geographically—we will acquire 100 customers in 90 days and establish the foundation for €1.2M ARR in Year 1.

The strategy is designed to be agile: we will double down on what works, quickly abandon what doesn't, and maintain our focus on the European technical founder segment that represents our highest-conversion, highest-retention opportunity.

**Next Steps**:
1. Execute pre-launch activities (Months -3 to 0)
2. Launch with focus on PLG + partnerships
3. Scale proven channels into Germany and UK
4. Build enterprise sales capability in Q3-Q4
