# CertFast Go-to-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

CertFast will launch with a phased approach targeting technical founders at B2B SaaS startups. Our go-to-market strategy combines product-led growth (PLG), strategic partnerships, and targeted outbound to reach €12M ARR by Year 3.

## Launch Timeline

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Months -2 to 0)

**Goals**: Build anticipation, validate product-market fit, establish partnerships

**Activities**:
- Launch landing page with waitlist
- Publish 10+ compliance guides and templates
- Recruit 5 design partners (free access in exchange for feedback)
- Sign 3 CPA firm partnerships
- Build email list of 500+ technical founders
- Create demo videos and case studies

**Success Metrics**:
- 500+ waitlist signups
- 5 committed design partners
- 3 signed CPA partnerships

### Phase 2: Launch (Month 1-3)

**Goals**: Generate first revenue, prove value proposition, gather testimonials

**Activities**:
- Public launch on Product Hunt, Hacker News, IndieHackers
- Limited availability (100 beta spots)
- Personalized onboarding for first customers
- Weekly webinars on compliance best practices
- Partner co-marketing with CPA firms

**Pricing**: 50% discount for first 100 customers (€99-499/month)

**Success Metrics**:
- 50+ paying customers
- €10K MRR
- 10+ customer testimonials
- 4.5+ product rating

### Phase 3: Growth (Months 4-12)

**Goals**: Scale customer acquisition, expand partnerships, reach profitability

**Activities**:
- Full self-serve onboarding
- Launch referral program
- Expand partnership network to 20+ CPA firms
- Content marketing scaling (2 blog posts/week)
- Webinar series with compliance experts
- Paid acquisition testing (LinkedIn, Google)

**Success Metrics**:
- 300+ paying customers
- €50K MRR
- 25% MoM growth
- Unit economics: CAC payback < 6 months

### Phase 4: Scale (Year 2-3)

**Goals**: Market leadership, enterprise expansion, international expansion

**Activities**:
- Enterprise tier launch (€2,999/month)
- SOC 2 automation (expand beyond ISO 27001, GDPR)
- European market expansion (local partnerships)
- Channel partnerships with cloud providers

**Success Metrics**:
- 1,900 customers
- €1M+ MRR
- 25% net margins
- 120%+ net revenue retention

## Channel Strategy

### 1. Product-Led Growth (Primary)

**Approach**: Self-serve freemium/trial model

**Mechanics**:
- 14-day free trial with full platform access
- Interactive product tour on landing page
- In-app guidance and compliance wizard
- Free compliance health check tool

**Why it works**: Technical founders prefer trying before buying, low-touch sales reduce CAC

### 2. Strategic Partnerships (Secondary)

**CPA Firm Partnerships**:
- Revenue share: 15% Year 1, 10% ongoing
- CPA firms recommend CertFast to clients
- Co-branded compliance templates
- Joint webinars and content

**Cloud Provider Partnerships** (Year 2):
- AWS, Azure, GCP marketplace listings
- Joint marketing for cloud-native startups
- Technical integration (SSO, audit logs)

### 3. Direct Sales (Enterprise)

**Trigger**: Companies with 50+ employees or €5M+ ARR

**Approach**:
- Dedicated account executive
- Custom compliance roadmap
- White-glove onboarding
- SLA guarantees

### 4. Content Marketing (Long-term)

**Strategy**: Become the authority on startup compliance

**Content Types**:
- Compliance guides by framework (SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR)
- Industry-specific templates (SaaS, fintech, healthtech)
- Founder stories and case studies
- Compliance cost calculators

**Distribution**: SEO, LinkedIn, newsletters, communities

## Sales Motion

### Self-Serve (Lite, Starter, Pro Tiers)

**Target**: Startups with 1-50 employees

**Journey**:
1. Land on website from content/partnership
2. Free trial sign-up (14 days)
3. Interactive onboarding wizard
4. First compliance milestone in 7 days
5. Convert to paid or churn

**Sales Cycle**: 0 days (product-led)
**CAC**: €100-300 (content/organic)

### Assisted Sales (Enterprise Tier)

**Target**: Companies with 50+ employees

**Journey**:
1. Demo request from website
2. Discovery call (30 min)
3. Custom compliance assessment
4. Proposal with implementation plan
5. Negotiation and close

**Sales Cycle**: 30-60 days
**CAC**: €3,000-5,000 (sales + marketing)

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Low trial conversion | Improve onboarding, add concierge |
| High churn | Better in-app guidance, customer success |
| Partnership delays | Multiple partner prospects, direct sales backup |
| Competitor response | Focus on speed (90 days), European market |
| Compliance changes | Close auditor relationships, continuous monitoring |
