## Executive Summary

This plan outlines the systematic acquisition strategy to onboard CertFast's first 100 paying customers. With an average contract value (ACV) of €2,400/year and a target CAC of €400, this plan projects €240,000 in ARR while maintaining healthy unit economics from day one.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Timeline**: 12 weeks post-launch
- **Target**: 100 paying customers
- **Weekly Target**: 8-9 new customers (ramping from 3 to 12)
- **Primary Channels**: Direct sales, partnerships, content marketing, referrals
- **Projected ARR**: €240,000

## 5. Referral Program Design

### "CertFast Champions" Referral Program

**Launch Date:** Week 8 (after initial customer base established)
**Target:** 25% of new customers from referrals by Week 12

#### Program Structure

**For Referrers (Existing Customers):**

| Tier | Referrals | Reward |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Advocate | 1 referral | €100 Amazon gift card OR €150 CertFast credit |
| Champion | 3 referrals | €400 total + Certified Champion badge |
| VIP | 5+ referrals | €800 total + exclusive swag + advisory council invite |

**For New Customers (Referred):**
- €200 off first year subscription
- Free compliance health check (€500 value)
- Priority onboarding (48-hour setup)

#### Program Mechanics

**Tracking:**
- Unique referral links in customer dashboard
- Automatic attribution via UTM + customer ID
- 90-day cookie window
- Real-time referral status tracking

**Fulfillment:**
- Rewards paid within 14 days of referred customer payment
- Credit applied automatically to next invoice
- Gift cards sent via email
- Monthly "Champions" newsletter with leaderboard

**Program Terms:**
- Referred customer must be new (not in database)
- Minimum 3-month subscription for reward eligibility
- No self-referrals or same-company referrals
- Rewards stack (no limit)

#### Launch Campaign (Week 8)

**Pre-Launch (Week 7):**
- Email teaser to all customers
- "Something special coming for our champions"
- Build anticipation

**Launch Day:**
- Personalized email to top 20 customers
- In-app notification for all users
- Social media announcement
- "Double rewards" launch promotion (Week 8-9 only)

**Ongoing Promotion:**
- Monthly referral reminders in newsletter
- Quarterly "Champion Spotlight" blog posts
- Slack community recognition
- Annual "Champion of the Year" award

#### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target Week 8-12 |
|--------|------------------|
| Program Enrollment | 60% of customers |
| Active Referrers | 25% of customers |
| Referrals per Active Referrer | 1.5 |
| Referral Conversion Rate | 40% |
| Total Referral Customers | 12 |
| Program CAC | €200 (vs €400 target) |

#### Viral Loop Design

```
Happy Customer → Shares Link → Friend Signs Up → Both Get Reward → 
Happy New Customer → Shares Link → [Repeat]
```

**Viral Coefficient Target:** 0.3 (every 10 customers bring 3 new ones)

## Conclusion

This 12-week acquisition plan provides a structured, multi-channel approach to acquiring CertFast's first 100 customers. By balancing direct sales, strategic partnerships, content marketing, and customer referrals, we create multiple growth engines that compound over time.

The phased approach allows for learning and optimization:
- **Weeks 1-4**: Validate sales motion and messaging
- **Weeks 5-8**: Scale what works, activate partnerships
- **Weeks 9-12**: Achieve sustainable growth through referrals

**Key Success Factors:**
1. Relentless focus on customer success (drives referrals)
2. Quick iteration on messaging based on feedback
3. Partnership quality over quantity
4. Content that genuinely helps EU startups navigate compliance
