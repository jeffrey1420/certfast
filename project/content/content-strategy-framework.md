## Executive Summary

This document defines CertFast's content strategy framework, translating our positioning as "the AI-native compliance platform for European technical founders" into a comprehensive editorial system. The strategy is designed to establish CertFast as the authoritative voice in European compliance automation, drive organic acquisition, and support our go-to-market objectives of acquiring 100 customers in 90 days post-launch.

**Strategic Objectives**:
1. Own the "European compliance" search category through SEO dominance
2. Establish thought leadership in EU regulatory developments (NIS2, DORA, GDPR)
3. Generate 5,000+ weekly website visits by Month 3
4. Support 40% of new customer acquisition through content-driven channels

## 4. Distribution Channel Strategy

### 4.1 Owned Channels

#### Blog (certfast.io/blog)
**Purpose**: Primary SEO channel and content hub
**Frequency**: 2 articles per week minimum
**Content Mix**: 60% educational, 25% thought leadership, 15% company news
**SEO Focus**: Target keywords, internal linking, featured snippets
**Success Metrics**: Organic traffic, time on page, conversion rate

#### Email Newsletter
**Purpose**: Nurture leads and build audience
**Frequency**: Weekly (Tuesday mornings)
**Segments**:
- Prospects: Educational content + product updates
- Customers: Advanced tips + feature releases
- Partners: Co-marketing opportunities
**Success Metrics**: Open rate (>25%), CTR (>3%), subscriber growth

#### Resource Center
**Purpose**: Gated content for lead generation
**Content**: Templates, checklists, calculators, guides
**Gating Strategy**: Email required for high-value downloads
**Success Metrics**: Conversion rate (>15%), lead quality score

---

### 4.2 Social Channels

#### LinkedIn (Primary B2B Channel)
**Purpose**: Thought leadership and community engagement
**Frequency**: 4-5 posts per week
**Content Mix**:
- 40% Educational content (framework tips, regulatory updates)
- 30% Company updates and milestones

## 7. Content Governance

### 7.1 Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone**: Professional but approachable, technical but accessible
**Voice Characteristics**:
- **Expert**: Demonstrates deep compliance knowledge
- **Empathetic**: Understands founder challenges
- **European**: Subtle cultural references, GDPR-first mindset
- **Technical**: Respects engineering intelligence
- **Direct**: No fluff, clear value propositions

**Language Guidelines**:
- Use British English (colour, organise)
- Include French terms where relevant ("mise en conformité")
- Avoid US-centric examples; use European case studies
- Use "we" for company, "you" for customer

### 7.2 Review Checklist

Before publishing, verify:
- [ ] Aligns with one of five content pillars
- [ ] Includes target keywords naturally
- [ ] Has clear call-to-action
- [ ] Technical accuracy verified by SME
- [ ] Brand voice consistent
- [ ] EU focus evident (not US-centric)
- [ ] Meta description optimized
- [ ] Internal links added
- [ ] Featured image included
- [ ] Mobile formatting checked

### 7.3 Content Refresh Schedule

| Content Type | Refresh Frequency |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Regulatory content | Quarterly (or on regulation change) |
| Framework guides | Every 6 months |
| Comparison content | Every 3 months |
| Customer stories | Annual update |
| Technical documentation | Continuous |
