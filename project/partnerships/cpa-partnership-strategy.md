## Executive Summary

This document outlines CertFast's comprehensive partnership strategy targeting Certified Public Accountant (CPA) firms across Europe, with an initial focus on France, Germany, and the United Kingdom. CPA partnerships represent our highest-leverage indirect sales channel, with a target of generating 30-40% of new customer acquisition by Year 2. The strategy is built on a three-tier partnership model (Anchor, Growth, Standard) that aligns incentives and provides differentiated value propositions for firms of varying sizes and influence.

Based on our unit economics analysis, CPA partnership customers have a 25% lower CAC compared to paid channels and convert at 2x the rate of organic leads, making this channel essential for sustainable growth toward our €1.2M ARR Year 1 target.

---

## 1. Partnership Vision & Objectives

### 1.1 Strategic Rationale

CPA firms are the ideal partnership target for CertFast for three critical reasons:

1. **Trust Transference**: Compliance decisions carry high stakes. When a trusted CPA recommends CertFast, the trust they've built with clients transfers to our platform, dramatically shortening sales cycles.

2. **Existing Relationships**: CPA firms already have the exact customer relationships we want—B2B SaaS companies preparing for audits. They are literally in the room when compliance decisions are made.

3. **Mutual Benefit**: CertFast makes CPAs' jobs easier by organizing evidence, automating policy creation, and reducing back-and-forth during audits. This efficiency gain makes them natural advocates.

### 1.2 Partnership Objectives (Year 1)

| Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Signed Partners | 10+ firms | Critical mass for consistent referral flow |
| Partner-Sourced Customers | 30% of total | Channel mix optimization per GTM strategy |

| Average Partner Referrals | 10/year/firm | Sustainable, not one-off introductions |
| Partner Satisfaction Score | >4.0/5.0 | Indicator of long-term relationship health |

## 3. Partnership Tier Structure

### 3.1 Three-Tier Model Overview

| Tier | Anchor | Growth | Standard |
|------|--------|--------|----------|
| **Target Profile** | Big 4/mid-tier, 100+ auditors | Regional players, 40-80 auditors | Boutiques, 15-35 auditors |
| **Commitment** | Exclusive co-marketing | Preferred referral agreement | Referral partnership |
| **Revenue Share** | 20% Year 1, 10% ongoing | 15% Year 1, 8% ongoing | 10% Year 1, 5% ongoing |
| **Minimum Referrals** | 20/year | 10/year | 5/year |
| **Training** | Quarterly in-person | Bi-annual virtual | On-demand online |
| **Co-marketing** | Joint events, PR, content | Case studies, webinars | Logo placement |
| **Support** | Dedicated partner manager | Email + quarterly calls | Email support |
| **Product Access** | Unlimited free licenses | 5 free licenses | 2 free licenses |

### 3.2 Tier Progression

Partners can progress between tiers based on performance:

- **Standard → Growth**: 8+ successful referrals in 12 months, partner satisfaction >4.0/5
- **Growth → Anchor**: 18+ successful referrals in 12 months, strategic value demonstrated
- **Tier Maintenance**: Partners dropping below minimum referrals for 6 months may be downgraded

### 3.3 Anchor Tier: Deep Partnership

**Profile**: Top-tier firms with significant influence and deal volume

**CertFast Commitments**:
- Dedicated Partner Success Manager (quarterly business reviews)
- Unlimited free CertFast licenses for internal use and client demos
- Priority feature development input (quarterly roadmap sessions)
- Joint go-to-market budget: €10,000/year for co-marketing activities
- Executive sponsorship (CertFast CEO quarterly calls)
- First access to new features and beta programs
- Co-branded sales materials and templates

**Partner Commitments**:
- Minimum 20 successful referrals per year
- CertFast positioned as preferred compliance platform
- Joint press releases for major customer wins (4/year minimum)
- Speaking opportunities at partner events (2/year)
- Annual partnership planning session

**Target Firms**: Exponens, KPMG France Tech Practice, Grant Thornton France, KPMG Germany, PWC Germany, BDO UK, RSM UK

### 3.4 Growth Tier: Strategic Partnership

**Profile**: Mid-tier firms with strong regional presence and tech focus

**CertFast Commitments**:
- Partner enablement specialist (bi-annual check-ins)
- 5 free CertFast licenses for internal use
- Access to partner portal with sales resources
- Joint case study development (2/year)
- Webinar co-hosting opportunities
- Early access to new features (2 weeks ahead)

**Partner Commitments**:
- Minimum 10 successful referrals per year
- CertFast included in compliance vendor recommendations
- Quarterly utilization review calls
- Participation in partner advisory board
- Feedback on product roadmap (quarterly survey)

**Target Firms**: BDO France Innovation, RSM France, ECFI, Exco Valleo, BDO Germany, Ebner Stolz, Warth & Klein, Mazars UK, Saffery Champness, haysmacintyre

### 3.5 Standard Tier: Referral Partnership

### 4.3 Definitions

- **"Qualified Referral"**: A customer introduced by Partner who (a) is not an existing CertFast customer, (b) completes a purchase within 90 days of referral, and (c) is properly registered in CertFast's partner tracking system.

- **"Net Revenue"**: Gross subscription revenue less discounts, refunds, chargebacks, and applicable taxes.

- **"Contract Term"**: Initial subscription period (monthly or annual) plus any renewals.

- **"Partner Tier"**: [ANCHOR/GROWTH/STANDARD] as determined by Company based on Partner's capabilities and commitments.

### 4.4 Referral Obligations

**4.4.1 Partner Responsibilities**:
- Maintain professional standards in all referrals
- Provide accurate customer information through partner portal
- Not make unauthorized representations or commitments on behalf of Company
- Maintain confidentiality of customer data per GDPR requirements
- Complete annual compliance training provided by Company

**4.4.2 Company Responsibilities**:
- Process referrals within 2 business days
- Provide Partner with referral status updates
- Deliver agreed-upon partner tier benefits
- Maintain accurate tracking and reporting of referral conversions
- Pay revenue share per Section 4.5

### 4.5 Revenue Share Terms

| Partner Tier | Year 1 Rate | Ongoing Rate | Payment Terms |
|--------------|-------------|--------------|---------------|
| Anchor | 20% of Net Revenue | 10% of Net Revenue | Quarterly, 30 days after quarter end |
| Growth | 15% of Net Revenue | 8% of Net Revenue | Quarterly, 30 days after quarter end |
| Standard | 10% of Net Revenue | 5% of Net Revenue | Quarterly, 45 days after quarter end |

**Year 1 Definition**: First 12 months of customer's subscription.
**Ongoing**: All subscription periods after Year 1, including renewals and expansions.

### 4.6 Tracking & Attribution

- Partner receives unique referral tracking links and partner codes
- Referrals must be registered before customer signs up to qualify
- Attribution period: 90 days from initial referral
- Disputes resolved based on CertFast CRM records (Salesforce)
- Monthly partner dashboard with referral status and commission calculations

### 4.7 Term & Termination

**Term**: Initial term of 12 months, auto-renewing unless terminated.

**Termination for Convenience**: Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.

**Termination for Cause**: Immediate termination for breach of confidentiality, misrepresentation, or violation of professional standards.

**Post-Termination**: Revenue share continues for customers referred during the term for the duration of their subscription.

### 4.8 Confidentiality & Data Protection

- Both parties maintain strict confidentiality of customer data
- Partner agrees to GDPR compliance and data processing requirements
- No customer data sharing without explicit consent
- Each party responsible for their own data security measures

### 4.9 Marketing & Brand Guidelines

- Partner may use CertFast logo per brand guidelines (provided separately)
- Co-marketing activities require written approval
- Partner cannot claim exclusivity or endorsement beyond agreement scope
- All public statements require mutual approval

### 4.10 Limitation of Liability

- Neither party liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages
- Company's total liability limited to revenue share paid in preceding 12 months
- Partner acknowledges referral decisions are at their sole discretion
- Company makes no guarantee of referral volume or revenue

### 4.11 General Provisions

- **Governing Law**: French law, with disputes resolved in Paris commercial courts
- **Amendment**: Written amendment only, signed by both parties
- **Assignment**: No assignment without written consent
- **Entire Agreement**: Supersedes all prior agreements
- **Severability**: Invalid provisions severable, remainder enforceable
