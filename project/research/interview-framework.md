## Interview Script Template

### Introduction (2 minutes)

> "Thank you for taking the time to speak with us. We're building CertFast, a compliance automation platform specifically designed for European startups. We're not selling anything today—we're conducting research to understand how early-stage companies think about SOC 2 and compliance. The interview will take about 30 minutes. Is that okay?"

### Section 1: Background & Context (5 minutes)

1. **Company Context**
   - Tell me about your company—what do you do, how many employees, what stage?
   - Who are your typical customers?
   - Are you selling to enterprise customers?

2. **Current Compliance Status**
   - What compliance frameworks do you currently have (SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR)?
   - If none, what's prevented you from pursuing compliance?
   - Have you lost deals because of compliance requirements?

### Section 2: Pain Points (8 minutes)

3. **Current Process**
   - If you've gone through compliance before, how did you do it?
   - How many hours did it take? Who was involved?
   - What was the most frustrating part of the process?

4. **Competitor Awareness**
   - Are you familiar with tools like Vanta, Drata, or Secureframe?
   - Have you evaluated any of them? What stopped you from moving forward?
   - What did you like/dislike about them?

### Section 3: Pricing Validation (7 minutes)

5. **Budget Expectations**
   - What would you expect to pay for a compliance automation tool?
   - [Show pricing: €199/299/499/999 per month] — What's your reaction?
   - Would you prefer monthly or annual billing? Why?

6. **Value Perception**
   - What would make this price feel like a "no-brainer"?
   - What features would need to be included at each tier?

### Section 4: Feature Priorities (5 minutes)

7. **Must-Have Features**
   - If you were buying a compliance tool today, what are your top 3 must-haves?
   - How important is AI-powered policy generation? (1-10)
   - How important is automated evidence collection? (1-10)
   - How important is having a French/European provider? (1-10)

8. **Timeline Expectations**
   - We promise "audit-ready in 90 days" — what do you think?
   - Is that believable? Desirable?

### Section 5: Closing (3 minutes)

9. **Feedback**
   - What did we not ask that we should have?
   - Would you be interested in being a design partner or early customer?
