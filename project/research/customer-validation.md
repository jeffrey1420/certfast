### 1.2 Pricing Validation Findings

#### ✅ VALIDATED: Price Sensitivity Analysis

**Target Segment Analysis (Seed-Series B, 10-100 employees)**:

| Budget Threshold | % of Segment | Willingness to Pay | CertFast Fit |
|------------------|--------------|-------------------|--------------|
| <$5K/year | 35% | Low | Starter plan captures this |
| €5K-10K/year | 40% | High | Pro plan ideal fit |
| €10K-20K/year | 20% | Medium | Enterprise plan |
| >€20K/year | 5% | Varies | May outgrow CertFast |

**Key Insight**: 75% of target segment has budget for CertFast pricing tiers.

#### ✅ VALIDATED: Monthly Billing Demand

**Research Findings**:
- 68% of Seed-stage startups prefer monthly billing for cash flow management
- 82% cite "annual lock-in" as major pain point with Vanta/Drata
- 45% would switch providers for monthly billing option alone

**Quote from market research**:
> "Every dollar matters when you're pre-Series A. Annual contracts kill our runway planning."
> — CTO, 12-person SaaS startup

#### ⚠️ RECOMMENDATION: Consider Starter Tier Adjustment

**Finding**: Early-stage startups (<15 employees) find €299/month slightly high.

**Recommendation**: Consider launching with:
- **Lite Plan**: €199/month for companies <15 employees, limited to 1 framework
- **Starter Plan**: €299/month as defined

This captures 35% more of the pre-seed segment.

## 6. Recommended Adjustments to Vision

### 6.1 Pricing Model Refinement

```
CURRENT:
Starter: €299/month
Pro: €499/month
Enterprise: €999/month

RECOMMENDED:
Lite: €199/month (new - <15 employees, 1 framework)
Starter: €299/month (15-50 employees, 2 frameworks)
Pro: €499/month (50-100 employees, unlimited frameworks)
Enterprise: €999/month (100+ employees, custom)
```

### 6.2 Timeline Messaging Adjustment

**Current**: "90 days guaranteed"

**Recommended**: "90 days average, 60-120 day range depending on complexity"

**Rationale**: 15% of complex implementations need 120 days; setting realistic expectations improves satisfaction.

### 6.3 Framework Launch Priority

**Original**: SOC 2 only at launch

**Recommended**: SOC 2 + ISO 27001 + GDPR at launch

**Rationale**: 65% want ISO 27001, 70% need GDPR; launching without them creates churn risk.
