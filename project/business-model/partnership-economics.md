## Executive Summary

This document analyzes the unit economics of CertFast's CPA firm partnership strategy. Based on market research indicating that **45% of prospects value audit bundling**, partnerships with 3 EU CPA firms could reduce CAC by 25% while accelerating revenue growth.

### Key Findings

| Metric | Direct Sales | Partnership | Improvement |
|--------|--------------|-------------|-------------|
| **CAC** | €2,400 | €1,800 | **-25%** |
| **Conversion Rate** | 12% | 24% | **+100%** |
| **Sales Cycle** | 45 days | 30 days | **-33%** |
| **LTV:CAC** | 10:1 | 13:1 | **+30%** |
| **Payback Period** | 6 months | 4.5 months | **-25%** |

**Recommendation**: Pursue partnerships with 3 EU CPA firms at **15% revenue share** with declining structure to **10%** for renewals.

## 1. Partnership Strategy Overview

### 1.1 Partnership Model

**Type**: Referral/Reseller Partnership with CPA Firms  
**Target Partners**: 3 EU-based CPA firms with startup focus  
**Geography**: France, Germany, Netherlands (primary EU markets)

**Partner Profile**:
- Mid-size firms (50-200 employees)
- Existing startup/tech client base
- Limited compliance automation offerings
- Interest in service bundling

### 1.2 Value Proposition for Partners

| Partner Benefit | Description |
|-----------------|-------------|
| **Revenue Share** | 15% of Year 1, 10% ongoing |
| **Differentiation** | Offer "tech-enabled" compliance |
| **Client Retention** | Stickier relationships via bundled service |
| **Efficiency** | Reduced manual compliance work |
| **Upsell Opportunity** | Audit services + CertFast subscription |

| Period | Revenue Share | Rationale |
|--------|---------------|-----------|

- Competitive with SaaS partnership norms (10-20%)

## 7. Risk Analysis

### 7.1 Partnership Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Partner underperforms** | Medium | Medium | 3-partner portfolio diversifies |
| **Partner conflicts** | Low | Medium | Clear territory/account definitions |
| **Margin erosion** | Low | Medium | Cap revenue share at 15% |
| **Channel conflict** | Medium | Low | Partners focus on audit-led deals |
| **Partner churn** | Medium | High | Annual commitments, exclusivity options |

### 7.2 Risk Mitigation Strategies

1. **Performance Tiers**: Partners must hit quarterly minimums
2. **Exclusivity Zones**: Geographic or vertical exclusivity
3. **Direct Oversight**: Joint account planning for top deals
4. **Competitive Parity**: Same pricing across all channels

## 9. Recommendations

### 9.1 Partnership Strategy

✅ **Pursue 3 CPA firm partnerships** with the following profile:
- Mid-size EU firms (50-200 employees)
- Strong startup/tech client base
- Willingness to co-sell and bundle

✅ **Offer 15%/10% revenue share structure**:
- 15% for Year 1 (attractive acquisition incentive)
- 10% for renewals (sustainable long-term)

✅ **Focus on audit bundling**:
- "Compliance + Audit" packages
- 20% discount on bundled audits
- Faster sales cycles, higher conversion

### 9.2 Partner Selection Criteria

| Criteria | Weight | Minimum Threshold |
|----------|--------|-------------------|
| **Startup Client Base** | 30% | 50+ startup clients |
| **Audit Volume** | 25% | 20+ SOC 2 audits/year |
| **Tech Readiness** | 20% | Willing to use platform |
| **Geographic Fit** | 15% | France/Germany/NL |
| **Reputation** | 10% | 4+ star reviews |

### 9.3 Key Terms for Partnership Agreements

1. **Revenue Share**: 15% Year 1, 10% ongoing
2. **Payment Terms**: Quarterly, net-30
3. **Minimums**: 5 deals/quarter after ramp
4. **Exclusivity**: Non-exclusive (protects against underperformance)
5. **Term**: 1 year with auto-renewal
6. **Termination**: 90-day notice

## 10. Appendix: Partner Financial Model

### 10.1 Partner Unit Economics Calculator

```
Partner Revenue Share = (Customer ARR × 15%) + Setup Fee

CertFast Net per Partner Customer = ARR - (ARR × 15%) - €150

Partner CAC = €150 (enablement) + €50 (marketing) = €200

Partner LTV:CAC = (Net ARR × 3 years) / €200
```
