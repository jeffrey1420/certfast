## 1. Revenue Model Overview

### 1.1 Pricing Structure (4 Tiers)

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | Target Segment | Employee Range |
|------|---------|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **Lite** | €199 | €1,990 | Pre-seed | <15 |

| **Pro** | €499 | €4,990 | Series A | 50-100 |
| **Enterprise** | €999 | €9,990 | Series B+ | 100+ |

### 1.2 Customer Distribution Assumptions

Based on market research, the expected customer mix evolves as follows:

| Year | Lite | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|------|------|---------|-----|------------|
| **Year 1** | 35% | 40% | 20% | 5% |
| **Year 2** | 30% | 35% | 25% | 10% |
| **Year 3** | 25% | 30% | 30% | 15% |

**Rationale**: As brand awareness grows, larger customers become easier to acquire, shifting mix toward higher tiers.

### 1.3 Billing Mix Assumptions

| Year | Monthly | Annual (upfront) |
|------|---------|------------------|
| **Year 1** | 60% | 40% |
| **Year 2** | 55% | 45% |
| **Year 3** | 50% | 50% |

**Rationale**: Larger customers prefer annual billing; as we move upmarket, annual % increases.

## 11. Recommendations

### 11.1 Pricing Strategy

1. ✅ **Launch with all four tiers** including Lite (€199)
2. ✅ **Maintain 2 months free** annual discount (16.7%)
3. ⚠️ **Monitor cannibalization** between Lite and Starter closely
4. 💡 **Consider dynamic pricing** for annual plans based on payment timing

### 11.2 Growth Strategy

1. **Prioritize product-led growth** for Lite tier — lowest CAC, fastest payback
2. **Invest in content/SEO** early — compound returns in Year 2-3
3. **Build partnerships with 3+ CPA firms** — 25% lower CAC
4. **Focus on annual billing** — improves cash flow to fund growth

### 11.3 Financial Management

1. **Track monthly**: CAC by channel, churn by tier, expansion revenue
2. **Review quarterly**: Pricing optimization, cost efficiency
3. **Plan for**: Series A at €1.5M ARR (end of Year 1)
